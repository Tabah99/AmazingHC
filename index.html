ABOUT US

We are in Herbal Cosmetics (cultivation/trading/export) business since 1935. Before partition of India we were doing henna cultivation / trading in Faridabad (India), and now this is the third generation of our family in Pakistan who is handling this ancestral business.
We have our Henna fields, out of them we are getting two crops per year around 500 tons of Henna leaves of fine quality having strong dying capacity, after giving full sunshine to Henna leaves, we impart all foreign particles from Henna leaves and grind these (only) cleaned leaves in our own factory, under strict supervision to maintain quality control.
